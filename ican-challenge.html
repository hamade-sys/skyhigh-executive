About the Challenge

Not a Case Study —
A Live Business Arena

Unlike traditional case competitions, participants engage in a dynamic digital business simulation where teams make real-time decisions, respond to shifting market conditions, and collaborate to achieve the strongest outcomes.

Designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world business environments through practical, experiential learning.

🧠

Strategic Decision-Making

Navigate complex scenarios with real consequences under competitive pressure.

🤝

Team Leadership & Collaboration

Build cohesive strategies, delegate effectively, and perform as a unit.

📊

Real-Time Analytics

Interpret live simulation data and pivot your strategy to outperform rivals.

The Competition Journey
1
Registration

Universities nominate one team of four undergraduates with a faculty advisor.

2
Briefing & Onboarding

Confirmed teams receive platform access and orientation sessions.

3
Simulation Rounds

Teams compete across multiple rounds making live business decisions.

4
Final Showcase

Top teams present strategies to industry judges for the title.

5
Awards Ceremony

UAE's top student business team crowned at a prestigious event.

Eligibility

Who Can Compete?

Open to university teams across the UAE meeting the following criteria.

🎓

Student Requirements

  • Currently enrolled in an undergraduate degree
  • Preferred: 3rd or 4th year of study
  • Nominated by their university
  • Available for all competition and briefing sessions
🏛️

University Requirements

  • Recognised UAE university or higher education institution
  • One team submission per university
  • Must include a faculty advisor or university representative
  • Submission of all required documentation and Emirates IDs
